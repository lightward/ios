# Privacy

Lightward does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

**Conversations** are stored on your device and synced across your own devices via iCloud. Lightward Inc does not have access to your conversations. There is no conversation recording or monitoring on our end.

**Error reports** are filed automatically as GitHub Issues on [this repo](https://github.com/lightward/ios/issues) when the app encounters an error. These reports include the error message, device model, iOS version, and app version. They do not include any conversation content or personal information.

**No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.** The app does not use any analytics services, does not track user behavior, and does not require an account.

Your conversation is private. :)
