# Privacy

## What data the app collects

The text of your conversation — your choices in the phoropter and your messages in chat. Nothing else.

## How it collects that data

You type it (or dictate it via voice-to-text). The app does not access contacts, location, photos, or any other data on your device.

## How the data is used

Your conversation text is sent to **Lightward AI** (lightward.com) for processing. Lightward AI uses **Anthropic's Claude API** to generate responses. Your conversation text is transmitted to these services in order to provide the conversational experience.

- **Lightward AI** does not store your conversations on its servers. Conversations are processed in real time and not retained.
- **Anthropic** processes API requests according to their [usage policy](https://www.anthropic.com/policies). Per Anthropic's API terms, data sent via the API is not used to train models.

## What stays on your device

Your conversation history is stored locally on your device and synced across your own devices via iCloud. Lightward Inc does not have access to your stored conversations. There is no conversation recording or monitoring on our end.

## Error reports

Error reports are filed automatically as GitHub Issues on [this repo](https://github.com/lightward/ios/issues) when the app encounters a technical error. These reports include the error message, device model, iOS version, and app version. They **do not** include any conversation content or personal information.

## No tracking

No analytics, no accounts, no advertising. The app does not use any analytics services and does not track user behavior.

Your conversation is private. :)
